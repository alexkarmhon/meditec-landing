    About Us

    We are a family clinic specializing in vertebrology, which has been treating patients with spine and joint pain, posture disorders, and other musculoskeletal health issues for over a hundred years.

    In the early 2000s, for our own needs, we developed a unique medicinal mud extract, which became the foundation for creating cosmetics with exceptional healing properties.

    Our latest developments were made in collaboration with Professor of reconstructive medicine, US Army medical officer, Dr. Antonio Toro. His many years of experience in helping civilians after mine and explosive injuries enabled us to create an incredibly effective scar-reducing agent, which is now the flagship of our product line.

    You can read patient reviews and research on our website.

    We are proud to donate part of our products to supply military medical units, supporting those who need it the most.
